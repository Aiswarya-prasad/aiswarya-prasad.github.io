--- layout: archive permalink: /categories/ title: "My Hobbies and Interests" author_profile: true --- This is a blog in the making—an exercise in organizing and reflecting on my interests, while also creating a space to share them with others who might enjoy the same things. Over the past few years, I've found that topics like gut microbes, South Indian cooking, Carnatic music, or a recent travel story almost always find their way into conversations. I enjoy going down deep dives—on Reddit, YouTube, or over an hour-long phone call with a friend—and love when these chats leave others just as curious or excited as I am. One of my favorite outcomes is seeing someone walk away newly interested in the gut microbiome (though hopefully not convinced that our microbes control *everything*—they don't!). Or when friends trying to eat more vegetarian meals ask for something tasty and practical—because despite the abundance of recipes online, it's hard to grasp an entire cuisine from scratch. I grew up in a vegetarian household in a place where that's the default. That experience gave me a deep library of dishes, habits, and tricks I now try to document and adapt for others, especially those cooking outside South Asia or new to the ingredients. This space is my attempt to share some of that, and a bit more.
